查经 – 耶稣是谁? 行神迹的神

Posted on October 14, 2011October 20, 2011 by utmccf

日期/DATE: 星期五，10月14日 Friday, October 14, 2011
时间/TIME: 6:00-8:00 pm
地点/LOCATION： GB 304

弟兄姊妹们平安：

在上周感恩节的活动中,我们一起学习和探讨了”感恩”这个话题. 就像经文中提到的另外九个麻风病人, 很多时候,我们常常忘记了感谢神的恩赐. 而感恩也不仅仅是在我们得到我们所求的时候,更要在我们没有得到应许的时候感恩，因为神可以通过苦难来建造和预备我们。

这 周五的活动中,我们将根据约翰福音中的一段经文来介绍耶稣究竟是谁.为什么耶稣能对那个回来感谢祂的人说” 你的信救了你”?  也许你听过有人说祂是神的独生爱子, 化成肉身来承担我们的罪, 可是我怎样才知道这是真的呢？祂究竟做过什么让历史上那么多人信靠祂? 信心是一点点地培养和累积的,而圣经中记载耶稣所行的神迹就是我们信心的一个重要部分.欢迎大家周五来加入我们的活动,通过读经和讨论,更深的了解和认识 我们的主—耶稣.

Dear brothers and sisters:

During our event last week, we learnt and discussed the topic of “thanksgiving”.  Just like the other nine people who got cured of leprosy but did not return to thank Jesus, we often forget to give glory to God for His blessings. Moreover, we should not only be thankful when we receive what we hope for, but also be thankful when obstacles are placed in our lives because God uses them to build our integrity and faith.

This Friday, we will learn more about who Jesus is through studying a Bible passage in the gospel of John. Why was Jesus able to say to the person that returned to thank him, “your faith has made you well”? Maybe you have heard people say that He is God’s only begotten son, who came to this world to die for our sins, but how do we know that this is true? What has He done that made so many people in history believe in Him? Faith is built up little by little and the miracles that Jesus performed in the Bible serve as a strong basis. Therefore, we welcome you all to come and join this week’s Bible study in order to better trust in Jesus Christ, our Lord.
