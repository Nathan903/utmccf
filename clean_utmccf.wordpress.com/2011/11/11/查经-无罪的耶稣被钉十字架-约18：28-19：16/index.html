查经 –无罪的耶稣被钉十字架 (约18：28 – 19：16)

Posted on November 11, 2011November 17, 2011 by utmccf

日期/DATE: 星期五，11月11日 Friday, November 11, 2011
时间/TIME: 6:00-8:00 pm
地点/LOCATION： SF 3201

弟兄姊妹们平安：

本 周五就是大家都知道的”神棍节”啦~~~~欢迎大光棍,小光棍们还有已经脱离光棍群体的同学们和我们一起过”节”~~其实,在我们感慨爱情的时候,不要忘 记,我们已经得到了这个世上最伟大的爱—那就是神对我们的爱!!神对我们的大爱早已记在圣经中,”神爱世人,甚至将祂的独生子赐给他们,叫一切信祂的,不 至灭亡,反得永生”.

在这周五的活动中,我们将继续根据约翰福音第18,19章中的部分经文来讨论耶稣为我们所做的牺牲.在这部分经文中, 耶稣和犹太人的行为和话语都被记录了下来, 在逾越节这个感谢神的日子, 神的儿子竟然被钉死在十字架上.为什么围观的犹太人宁愿选择释放一个强盗, 却不愿放了传道, 行神迹的耶稣? 耶稣用自己的生命应验了旧约的预言，也为世人的罪被钉十架. 欢迎大家在周五继续加入我们,和我们一起纪念耶稣基督的牺牲.

Dear brothers and sisters:

As the season of fall hits home, we are seeing flashes of red poppies worn on the coats of many people we encounter. That is right. This Friday is Remembrance Day. On this day we remember those who have made life sacrifices in past wars. We remember them, for without them, we wouldn’t be able to enjoy the peace in our society today.  However, at the same time, we should not forget the One who made the ultimate sacrifice for all of us; who loved us enough to die for us on the cross. It is through Jesus that we can experience the peace from within our hearts. It is through His pain and sufferings that we are healed.

This Friday, we will continue in the book of John chapters 18 and 19 to learn of the sacrifice that Jesus made for us. In these two chapters, we will read in detail how He, the Son of God, was nailed on the cross for us. Why was the crowd so willing to let free of a murderer, yet so willing to crucify Jesus, who had done nothing wrong? He fulfilled the prophesies of the Old Testament, and He gladly laid down His life for our sins. We invite you all to join us in this week’s Bible study so we remember Him, who is the ultimate sacrifice for you and me.
