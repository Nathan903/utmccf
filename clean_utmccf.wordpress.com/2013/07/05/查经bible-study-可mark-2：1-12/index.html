查经/Bible Study (可/Mark 2：1-12)

Posted on July 5, 2013July 10, 2013 by utmccf

親愛的弟兄姊妹們,

由於得到高人指引，這週的News Letter改頭換面，煥然一新！本週五我們還是會在Multifaith Center 二樓 KP Activity Hall進行我們的查經活動。這次我們有幸請到了 多倫多華人福音堂 的 黃智奇 牧師 來帶領我們查考馬可福音2：1-12 （此處應該有掌聲！）熱烈歡迎新老朋友參加～

禱告會:
週五 5:00 pm – 6:00 pm
Multifaith Centre 2nd Floor KP Activity Hall

團契聚會:
週五 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm
Multifaith Centre 2nd Floor KP Activity Hall

Dear Brothers and Sisters,

How’s the new look of our News Letter?!!! XD This week, we will have Rev. Wong from Chinese Gospel Church coming in to lead the Bible study Mark 2:1-12. Hope to see you there!!

Fellowship:
Friday 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm
Multifaith Centre 2nd Floor KP Activity Hall

Prayers Meeting:
Friday 5:00 pm – 6:00 pm
Multifaith Centre 2nd Floor KP Activity Hall
