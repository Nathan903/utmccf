新学期的活动/New School Term Meetings

Posted on August 17, 2012September 13, 2012 by utmccf

愉快而激动人心的Summer马上就要结束了,在恭喜各位顺利完成Final Exam的同时,我们也不得不桑心的告诉大家,我们暑假的活动已经结束了.预祝各位在暑假剩余的时间里过的开心而充实,虽然我们团契的活动暂停了,但我们的关心和祝福不会因此远离的大家的!!

我们新学期的活动会从9月14号开始, 欢迎大家踊跃的加入我们,当然也可以把你的朋友和同学带到我们的中间,一起分享从神而来的祝福和喜乐~~同时,在Frosh Week的周三,也就是9月5号,我们会在King’s Circle有一个宣传活动, 欢迎有时间也有意愿的同学加入我们,愿意来帮忙的同学可以和直接回复我们,我们会尽快和你联系,安排具体的事情.

开学后我们MCCF会继续期待每周五与大家的约会滴~~

Dear brothers and sisters:

Congratulations to those who have finished their exams and are chilling (literally) in this cool summer weather! We hope you all did well (if not, just don’t check your marks! Hehe)

Sadly, last Friday was our last weekly fellowship meeting for the summer. Our new school year weekly meeting will begin on September 14th, the first Friday of Fall term. As always, we welcome everyone to join us, and we would be more than glad to have you and your friends (including BFF, or BF, or GF, you know what I mean =P) together with us, to share and to cherish a happy time in the presence of our Lord~

The next upcoming event is the Club Day on September 5th, the Wednesday of Frosh Week. We will be hosting booth for our fellowship at King’s Circle in front of Hart House, and we would like all of you to join us! Help is always needed, and new friendships are always welcomed~ For those who would like to help, please reply to this email. We will contact you shortly and let you know your task for the day~

Tis all for now, may God bless you to have an awesome time for the rest of the summer! See you on the 14th~
