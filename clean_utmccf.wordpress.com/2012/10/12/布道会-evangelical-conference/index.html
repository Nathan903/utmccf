布道会/ Evangelical Conference

Posted on October 12, 2012May 16, 2023 by utmccf

“如果我的出现,只是一个意外的巧合,为什么我渴望被爱?”
“如果你的出现,只是一个意外的巧合,我不会用性命来换!”

-小羊诗歌<<如果>>

兄弟姐妹们平安~~

上一周的火鸡大家吃得很开心吧!!还有更开心的~~那就是我们期待已久的布道会来了!欢迎大家带着自己的朋友来~~那我们相约在周五,不见不散!

本周活动/ Events This Week: 

1. 布道会/ Evangelical Conference (October 12th, 7 pm – 9 pm)
2. 生命小组/ Life Group (October 12th, 2 pm – 4 pm)

题目/THEME： 人生可以更精彩吗？
日期/DATE: 星期五，10月12日/ Friday, OCT 12TH, 2012
时间/TIME: 7:00 pm – 9:00 pm
地点/LOCATION: Earth Sciences Auditorium (ES1050)

好吃的吃多了或许没有味道，好玩的玩多了或许缺乏新鲜感，轻松拥有过的东西或许并不感到珍惜。丰富的物质条件和忙碌的生活节奏为何总是不能填补内心空虚？拥有理想却遥不可及？我的生命难道就不能更加精彩一些吗？

UTMCCF 是我们的家，这星期我们请到的是一位著名的科学家冯秉诚博士，希望从他的信息让你认识到更加精彩的生命。UTMCCF国语福音布道会《我还能活的更精彩吗？》等你来参加…

Food doesn’t seem as delicious as it once was, games are no longer entertaining, and things you once valued seems to carry no meaning anymore. Although your life is busy, you feel empty inside. You have dreams, but they are only dreams… is there more to life?

UTMCCF is the place that many of us have found answers to these problems. On October 12th we will be inviting a famous scientist and pastor Dr. Bingcheng Feng to share his experiences with us. This conference might help you answer some of your questions and reveal to you a different world where love, life, and purpose walk hand-in-hand. UTMCCF Mandarin Evangelical Conference “Is there more to life?” will be expecting you!

生命小组 /  Life Group
日期/DATE: 星期五，10月12日 / Friday, OCT 12TH , 2012
时间/TIME: 2:00 pm – 4:00 pm
地点/LOCATION: Gerstein Library (study room 2105)

生命小组是我们新增加的一个活动，在今后的每周五都会在同一时间，同一地点进行。这里将会帮助基督徒朋友们加深对圣经的学习和理解，在灵命上有所成长，也同样帮助未信主的朋友们更加了解基督教，了解耶稣的救恩，了解自己。相信我们~这里将会是让你受益匪浅，同时又倍感温馨的小课堂~~~

Life group is a new activity. It will be held every Friday in Gerstein study room. If you are a Christian, this can be a good opportunity for you to go deeper into the Bible alongside other fellowship members. If you are not a Christian, you are also welcome, since it might be able to help you know more about the Bible and God~~~~believe us, it will be a great experience of learning and sharing!!
