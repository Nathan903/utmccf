查经 / Bible Study – (可/Mark 8: 27-38)

Posted on July 26, 2013August 14, 2013 by utmccf

亲爱的弟兄姐妹们:

让人窒息的闷热天气总算是告一段落了！你剩下的假期打算怎么过呢？（对不起，不是故意提起这种令人伤感的话题。。。才怪～）这周五，对，没错！就是今天！我们会一起查考 马可福音 8章27-38节。请来 GB 405 下午六点，团长会等你哟！不见不散～ 😀

團契聚會:
週五, July 26,  6:00 pm – 8:00 pm
GB 405

禱告會:
週五 5:00 pm – 6:00 pm
GB405
Dear Brothers and Sisters,

Aren’t you glad that the humid weather had finally came to a stop?  Come to GB 405 this week at 6:00pm to study the words of God. This week, we will be looking atMark 8:27-38. All are welcome!!X3

Fellowship:
Friday, July 26, 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm
GB 405

Prayers Meeting:
Friday 5:00 pm – 6:00 pm
GB405
