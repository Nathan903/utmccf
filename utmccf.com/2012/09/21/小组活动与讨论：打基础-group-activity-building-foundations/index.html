小组活动与讨论：打基础 / Group Activity: Building Foundations

Posted on September 21, 2012September 27, 2012 by utmccf

很快又到了我们每周一次的幸福聚会时间!!还记得上周五我们认识了很多可爱的新朋友，也见到了我们思念已久的老朋友，并且大家一起度过了一个充满欢声笑语的夜晚！更加美好的是，我们的快乐即将持续，兄弟姐妹们一起期待接下来的这个周五吧！！

这周五我们还会有小组游戏，在游戏的同时领会圣经中的话语（马太7：24-27），之后进行讨论。我们期待着和新老朋友们的重聚，也期待着更多新朋友的加入。让我们在快乐中一起学习神的话语，一起在神的看顾下度过难忘的时光！

我们要学习的经文:

“所以凡听见我这话就去行的，好比一个聪明人，把房子盖在了磐石上，雨淋，水冲，风吹，撞着那房子，房子总不倒塌，因为根基立在磐石上。凡听见我这话不去行的，好比一个无知的人，把房子盖在沙土上。雨淋，水冲，风吹，撞着那房子，房子就倒塌了，并且倒塌的很大。” — 马太7：24-27

 

It’s almost time for our weekly gathering again!! Last Friday was full of cheer and joy as we met a lot of lovely new friends and reconnected with many “old” friends! Now the good news is that this sense of joy will continue with our next gathering!!! Let us look forward to this coming Friday~~

This week, we will be doing an activity together as well as having a group discussion centered on a passage from the Bible (Matthew 7:24-27). We sincerely invite you and your friends to join us!

The central passage:

“Therefore everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock. The rain came down, the stream rose, and the winds blew and beat against the house; yet it did not fall, because it had its foundation on the rock. But everyone who hears these words of mine and does not put them into practice is like a foolish man who built his house on sand. The rain came down, the streams rose, and the winds blew and beat against the house, and it fell with great crash.”  — Matthew 7:24-27
