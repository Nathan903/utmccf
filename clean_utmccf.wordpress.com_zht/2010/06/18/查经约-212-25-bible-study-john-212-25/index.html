查經(約 2:12-25) Bible Study (John 2:12-25)

Posted on June 18, 2010June 23, 2010 by utmccf

日期/DATE: 星期五，6月18日 Friday, June 18, 2010
時間/TIME: 6:00-8:00p.m
地點/LOCATION:
GB412

能一起查看神的道是我們的福分。基督曾 說過，無論在哪裏，有兩三個人奉我的名聚會，那裏就有我在他們中間。在這周的經文，約2：12-25，我們會學到耶穌爲什麼在逾越節潔淨耶路撒冷的聖殿。 通過這件 事，我們不光會了解到耶穌的權威，而且對我們自己的生活也會有所監察。希望大家不要錯過這機會來深入瞭解本段經文。

We are very fortunate to be able to study the Word of God together.  Christ once said, where two or three come together in my name, there am I with them.  In this week’s verses, John 2:12-25, we will learn why Jesus cleared the temple in Jerusalem during the Jewish Passover celebration.  Through the event, we will not only learn about the authority of Jesus, but will also become more critical of our own life.  Hope you do not miss this opportunity to study the passage in depth.
