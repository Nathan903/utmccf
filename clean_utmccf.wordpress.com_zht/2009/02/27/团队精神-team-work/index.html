團隊精神 Team Work

Posted on February 27, 2009May 3, 2017 by utmccf

日期/DATE: Friday, February 27, 2009
時間/TIME: ６:00-8:00p.m
地點/LOCATION: BA2165  (Map)

這周是TEAMWORK時間，團隊精神無處不在，希望大家能通過這次團契時間有所收益，相信大家在以後的任何團隊活動都得心應手。

This week, T.K. will share his expertise in Teamwork with us. Hope everyone can discover the importance of teamwork in our everyday life and learn to apply it.
