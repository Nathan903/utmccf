多倫多大學國語基督徒團契 University of Toronto Mandarin Chinese Christian Fellowship

UTMCCF Walkathon: Toronto → Niagara Falls

我們是誰？

多倫多大學基督徒團契每年在校園內查經、敬拜、小組和各樣活動，幫助30~40位留學生接觸福音，加深信仰，找到屬靈的家，建立真摯的友誼。爲了支持團契在新學年能更好地開展校園福音事工、服務更多同學，我們希望用汗水與腳步（痛苦與整活）來爲團契籌集資金。

我們要做什麼？

本週二（6月16日），團契11位同學將開始一項極限挑戰：從多倫多徒步四天三夜130公里，到尼亞加拉大瀑布（Niagara Falls）！

請求支持

我們希望以每公里捐款$3的方式，邀請大家來認領我們的里程！我們將自費承擔所有個人開支（食宿、止痛藥、水泡貼等），確保您的每一分捐款100%用於您所支持的項目。本次籌款共有兩個去向供您選擇：

Who are we?

Every year, the University of Toronto Mandarin Chinese Christian Fellowship runs Bible studies, worship, small groups, and various activities on campus to help 30~40 international students encounter the gospel, deepen their faith, find a spiritual home, and build genuine friendships. To support the fellowship in better serving more students in the new school year, we hope to raise funds through our sweat and footsteps (pain and memes).

What are we doing?

This Tuesday (June 16), 11 students from the fellowship will embark on an extreme challenge: walking 130 kilometers over four days and three nights from Toronto to Niagara Falls!

Request for Support

We hope to invite everyone to sponsor our mileage with a donation of $3 per kilometer! We will bear all personal expenses (food and accommodation, painkillers, blister patches, etc.) at our own expense, ensuring that 100% of your donation will be directly used for the project you support. There are two destinations for you to choose from:

選項一：支持多大團契校園事工

您的捐款將支持團契的發展：

  • 20% 用於團契日常運營
  • 10% 用於福音小分隊運營
  • 45% 支持多大團契輔導 Betty（CCEF校園宣教士）
  • 25% 支持CCEF 2027校園精兵營以及補貼報名費

*注：團契事工無法提供抵稅收據。

選項二：支持本地慈善機構 Yonge Street Mission (YSM)

您給YSM的捐款將用於消除多倫多的長期貧困，爲多倫多街頭的流浪青年、弱勢兒童和低收入家庭提供食物、醫療和就業支持等：

  • 提供熱餐、food bank、禦寒衣物及緊急安置。
  • 設立免費牙科診所，並提供心理輔導和創傷/成癮治療。
  • 提供免費托兒（幫助父母求職）、電腦技能培訓、就業對接，幫助他們自立更生。

Option 1: Support UTMCCF & CCEF Campus Ministry

Your donation will support the fellowship's development:

  • 20% for UTMCCF daily operations
  • 10% for UTMCCF gospel outreach
  • 45% to support our counselor Betty (CCEF missionary)
  • 25% to support CCEF 2027 CB Camp and subsidize registration fees

*Note: We are unable to provide donation tax receipts.

Option 2: Support Local Charity - Yonge Street Mission (YSM)

Your donation to YSM will be used to end chronic poverty in Toronto, providing food, healthcare, and employment support for homeless youth, vulnerable children, and low-income families:

  • Provide hot meals, food banks, winter clothing, and emergency shelter.
  • Operate free dental clinics, and provide counseling and trauma/addiction treatment.
  • Provide free childcare (helping parents job search), computer skills training, and employment matching to help them become self-reliant.
