徒步去瀑布：多大團契130km籌款挑戰

我們是誰？

多倫多大學基督徒團契每年在校園內查經、敬拜、小組和各樣活動，幫助30~40位留學生接觸福音，加深信仰，找到屬靈的家，建立真摯的友誼。爲了支持團契在新學年能更好地開展校園福音事工、服務更多同學，我們希望用汗水與腳步（痛苦與整活）來爲團契籌集資金。

我們要做什麼？

本週二（6月16日），團契8位同學將開始一項極限挑戰：從多倫多徒步四天三夜130公里，到尼亞加拉大瀑布（Niagara Falls）！

請求支持

我們希望以每公里捐款$3的方式，邀請大家來認領我們的里程！我們將自費承擔所有個人開支（食宿、止痛藥、水泡貼等），確保您的每一分捐款 100% 直接用於以下事工：

Who are we?

Every year, the University of Toronto Mandarin Chinese Christian Fellowship runs Bible studies, worship, small groups, and various activities on campus to help 30~40 international students encounter the gospel, deepen their faith, find a spiritual home, and build genuine friendships. To support the fellowship in better serving more students in the new school year, we hope to raise funds through our sweat and footsteps (pain and memes).

What are we doing?

This Tuesday (June 16), 8 students from the fellowship will embark on an extreme challenge: walking 130 kilometers over four days and three nights from Toronto to Niagara Falls!

Request for Support

We hope to invite everyone to sponsor our mileage with a donation of $3 per kilometer! We will bear all personal expenses (food and accommodation, painkillers, blister patches, etc.) at our own expense, ensuring that 100% of your donation will be directly used for the following ministries:

  • 20% 用於團契日常運營
  • 10% 用於福音小分隊運營
  • 45% 支持多大團契輔導 Betty
  • 25% 支持CCEF 2027校園精兵營以及補貼報名費
  • 20% for UTMCCF daily operations
  • 10% for UTMCCF gospel outreach
  • 45% to support our counselor Betty
  • 25% to support CCEF 2027 CB Camp and subsidize registration fees

  • 求神帶領整個行程中的天氣、住宿和交通安排
